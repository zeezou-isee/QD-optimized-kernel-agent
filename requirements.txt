#     the converted .ncnn.{param,bin} in-process to do an allclose vs torch.
#     Without it, the existence-check falls back to "structural only" and
#     reports a lot of false negatives.
#   - openai is used by llm_api.py for BOTH DeepSeek and OpenRouter (both
#     expose OpenAI-compatible endpoints).
